NiceFlamingo's Special Chicken

Description

A quick dinner that is sure to please everyone in your household. Perfect with a side of veggies and mac n cheese. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast or Chicken Breast Tenders
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Gently tenderize chicken breast or tenders.
  2. Mix Mayonnaise and Dijon Mustard to taste.
  3. Coat the chicken in the dressing mixture. Then top with bread crumbs.
  4. Gently press bread crumbs onto chicken on all sides.
  5. Lay chicken on a foil-lined sheet pan and bake at 350 degrees for 18-30 minutes depending on size and width of chicken.
  6. Enjoy
