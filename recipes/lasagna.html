NiceFlamingo's Lasagna

Description

A wonderful anytime meal. Brought to you by me. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • 1 lb Beef
  • 1 lb Pork
  • White or Red Wine - 1 cup
  • 2 Cans Preferred Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Half White Onion - Chopped Fine-ish
  • Ricotta - 15oz
  • 1 egg
  • Mozzarella
  • Lots of Good Parm

Steps

  1. Salt and pepper the meat and brown in a pan in batches. Drain oil/fat
  2. Cut lasagna sheets to fit pan size
  3. Simmer sweat garlic with onions then add to browned meat and sauce simmer for 1 hour or more
  4. Mix egg with Ricotta, pepper, and parm
  5. Spoon thin layer of sauce onto bottom of pan then lay first sheet
  6. Spoon Ricotta plops onto each layer, add sauce and Mozzarella
  7. Layer & Repeat
  8. Bake at 375 degrees for 30 min. Rotate pan half way through bake.
  9. Add Mozzarella cheese layer in last 10min of bake
  10. Place under broiler until brown and bubbling
  11. Pull from oven. Rest for 10 minutes
  12. Enjoy
