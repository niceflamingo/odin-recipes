NiceFlamingo's Potato Salad

Description

A summer time staple. Perfect addition to any summer BBQ or Picnic. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • Eggs
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil Potatoes to desired consistency. Longer for smoother Potatos or shorter for Cubed style.
  2. Allow to cool then cut into cubes.
  3. Hard boil eggs - Once water hits a boil, turn off burner, cover pot with lid and allow to sit for 12 minutes. After, remove from pot and transfer eggs into an ice bath.
  4. Chop celery & onion
  5. Mix cooled potatoes and eggs together with celery, onion, Mayonnaise and mustard.
  6. Add Salt & Pepper to taste.
  7. Enjoy
